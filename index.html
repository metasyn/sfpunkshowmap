SF Show Map

You can select which dates you are interested in on the bottom.
The box below that will give you details of the on-screen shows.
Clicking on a venue with a number will split out the individual shows.
Clicking on one will give you details of the lineup and cost, if known.

Legend

      *    recommendable shows
      a/a  all ages
      $    will probably sell out
      @    pit warning
      ^    under 21 must buy drink tickets   
      #    no ins/outs

All of this data comes from The List.
You might've noticed that some shows are missing.
I've only added a subset of venues so far.
